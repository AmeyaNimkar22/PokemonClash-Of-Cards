Instructions

Objective:

Win the most rounds by selecting powerful Pokémon cards to battle against the computer.

How to Play:

Start the Game:
Click on the "Play" button to begin.
Round Setup:
The game consists of 3 rounds. Each round, both you and the computer will draw a card.
Drawing Cards:
The computer will randomly generate a card for itself. You will choose a card from your hand to play against the computer’s card.
Playing a Card:
Select a card from your hand to play. Your chosen card will face off against the computer’s card.
Winning a Round:
The card with the higher value or better stats wins the round. The winner of each round will be displayed.
End of Game:
After 3 rounds, the player with the most round wins is declared the overall winner. The final score will be shown, indicating how many rounds you won versus the computer.

Instructions and Restart:

Click the "Instructions" button if you need a refresher on how to play. Restart the game by clicking the "Play" button again. Good Luck and Have Fun!
